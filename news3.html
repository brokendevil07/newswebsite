Mission Shakti 3.0 : CM Yogi to honour women for their extraordinary achievements.

‘Mission Shakti’ 3.0 is all set to be launched by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath on August 21, 2021, in Lucknow. The flagship programme, ‘Mission Shakti’ has set a new landmark in women’s empowerment. Ensuring safety, security and dignity, the state government stands by the motive to safeguard women’s rights.
What’s new in Mega ‘Mission Shakti’ 3.0?
1. Focused on the all-round development of the women of the state, the third and entirely new phase of ‘Mission Shakti’ now aims at making women self-reliant and self-independent.

2. CM Yogi-led Uttar Pradesh Government will honour women health workers, self-help groups, doctors, entrepreneurs and also women belonging to voluntary organisations for their extraordinary achievements in the field of women empowerment.

3. Around 75 women, from every district of the state, will be honoured by the state government under the ‘Mission Shakti' campaign.

4. Nodal head of the mission, Laxmi Singh (IPS) said the third phase of ‘Mission Shakti’ aims at providing police service at the doorstep of every woman, including those living in the rural areas.

All about the event ‘Mission Shakti’ 3.0

‘Mission Shakti’ 3.0 will be launched on August 21, 2021, in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. All the preparations for the event have already been completed at the administration level. Interestingly, Union Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman will attend the inauguration of the ‘Mission Shakti’ 3.0 in Lucknow.
CM Yogi and other senior government officials will also participate in the event. A grand event will be organised in every district of Uttar Pradesh but the main function will be held in Lucknow. The event will be held on the eve of Raksha Bandhan and that is why all the women taking part in the event will be given a pack of Rakhi and a mask as a gift from the Government.
